Who should I look towards when looking for art advice on youtube?

It's incredibly easy to feel lost on which youtubers to start watching. That's completely reasonable, since there are so many of them and they all have different methods
Luckily, we have this page to try and bring some guidance on very useful teachers that can help you reach your full artistic potential

General Art guides

For teachers that provide general guidance, we have people such as Marc Brunet, a former Blizzard designer, who even went as far as making marathon guides on how to get started with art in about 30 days
We also have Proko, who hosts his own art course, but gives plenty of guidance through his youtube channel! Be sure to check them out!


Other teachers that could be good for the beginners would be people such as Chommang Drawing, who makes dedicates a bunch of his videos to anatomy, structure and art tips! Same thing with Hodraw Official!



If I had to pick one to start with though, I'd definitely pick Marc Brunet
